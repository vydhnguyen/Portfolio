Experiments & Learning

This section highlights experiments and learning projects related to artificial intelligence, automation, and knowledge work. The goal of these projects is not polished production software, but rather thoughtful exploration of how emerging tools may reshape operational work.

AI Research Assistant Workflow

Problem Statement

Operators and leaders consume large amounts of information through books, podcasts, and articles, but much of that knowledge remains unstructured and difficult to act on later.

Hypothesis

A structured research workflow combining AI tools and knowledge management systems could help synthesize ideas and generate deeper insights from consumed material.

Approach

Experimented with tools such as Readwise, knowledge management systems, and AI assistants to organize quotes, ideas, and research notes from multiple sources into a unified system.

Outcome

Developed a workflow that enables consistent collection of highlights from multiple sources and supports active idea synthesis rather than passive consumption.

Lessons Learned

AI can significantly improve the process of connecting ideas across sources and generating structured insights. The bottleneck is not information access but information synthesis — and this is where AI provides the most leverage for knowledge workers.
